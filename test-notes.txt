What to test in this build:

1. Audio routing with EarPods/Bluetooth
   - Connect EarPods or Bluetooth headphones before opening the app
   - Start counting - audio should play through the connected device
   - Disconnect EarPods while counting - audio should switch to iPhone speaker
   - Reconnect EarPods - audio should switch back to the connected device

2. Audio routing without external devices
   - Start counting without any EarPods/Bluetooth connected
   - Audio should play through iPhone speaker at normal volume

3. Warm-up behavior
   - App should show "Calentando..." on launch (gray button, disabled)
   - After 1-3 seconds, button should change to "Vamos" and become enabled
   - Swipe gestures should not work during warm-up

4. First utterance clarity
   - Tap "Vamos" and listen to "Cero" - it should be complete, not cut off

5. Swipe acceleration
   - Swipe left/right to navigate numbers
   - Swipe multiple times quickly in the same direction - the jump should increase
   - Pause for 1+ second, then swipe again - should reset to +1

6. No vibration during counting
   - Start counting and hold the phone - no vibration should be felt
   - Place phone on a table while counting - no vibration should be heard/felt
